Modified Battleship (NAVAL WAR)

So we decided to modify Battleship. We saw that Battleship had some pretty big flaws to the original game. Flaws such as the lack of defensive capabilities, variety of attack options, and movement capabilities. We decided to focus on these flaws and add them to the game. Firstly, we felt that the attacks could have been a bit more creative in the original game. So, with that we add different attack option in the game. The player can attack on a cell by cell basis as in the original game, however the player is now also given the highly recommended option of drawing and assault card. These cards focus on the offensive side of Battleship and offer the play a variety of attacks. Attacks such as Airstrikes, Drone Strikes, Task Force attacks and more. Some of these attacks focuses on a single cell while others target a whole column of cells. We felt that this would make the game more interesting and fun.

However, to counter the OP offensive we had to create a stronger deffensive. Traditionally Battleship uses the unknown or "Fog of War" as the deffense. To us, that wasn't good enough. Battleship lacks redemption from ship attacks and discovery. So we created a larger board, add a new ship, and created movement and intel cards. The new game board is roughly 50% larger than the original. The new ship is called the Flag Ship (If the flag ship is sunk than the game is over.) Intel cards such as Smokescreens which creates non-combat zones and Engineering which regenerates health to ships allows the player to redeem themselves and tips the scales of the game. Movement cards amplifies the "Fog of War" effect in the game. My personal favorite of the movement cards is the Fleet Relocation card. This card allows the player to reposition all of their standing ships.
